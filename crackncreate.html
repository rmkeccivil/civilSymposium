CRACK n CREATE

Crack and create is an event in which there will be of two rounds

ROUND 1 :

1.It is a face-to-face round.

2.A question will be given to both teams which will have multiple answers.

3.Whoever taps the bench first will get the first turn.

4.If team A answers right they will be given the next chance Or else the chance passes on to the next team

5.The Person who has said most of the answers will be announced as the winner and will be qualified to the next round

ROUND 2:

1.A prototype of a model will be displayed to the participants.

2.The participants are expected to do a replica of the model.

3.The replica will be checked with loads.

All The Best...
