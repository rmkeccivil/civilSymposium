POSH PAPER

1.Each presenter will get 10 min for a presentation and 5 minutes for Q & A.

2.The presentation should be only in MS PowerPoint (ppt /pptx) or PDF formats.

3.The first slide should display the title of the presentation & name of the presenter.

4.The topic can be anything oriented toward civil engineering.

5.The presenter will be provided with an LED screen and a USB drive (if required).

6.Presenters are requested to send their presentations in advance to avoid any last-minute technology issues.
